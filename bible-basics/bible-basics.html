Bible Basics

This section of the website aims to help those who are seeking to understand some of the fundamentals of being a Christian. The short articles given here are primarily the results of private studies into the scriptures by several people.

In addition there are also a number of extra articles:

  • Questions: These are responses to questions and are added to the relevant subject.
  • Additional Reading: These are other articles (with writer’s names given) that were appreciated by some of us and may help expand on some of the subjects studied in the short papers.

It is probably best not to try to read all articles at once but to read the articles of interest that will help you with where you are in your spiritual journey at the moment. It may be a good idea to start by reading the articles on “Salvation”.

Articles are currently being changed from PDF format to HTML and then being uploaded to this section of the website. This should be completed shortly. Please keep checking for new articles.
