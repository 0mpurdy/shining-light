Frequently asked questions

There are a number of ‘Frequently Asked Questions’ about the gospel below. Hopefully, they will be able to answer all the questions you have about what God has done to redeem us through the death and resurrection of His Son, Jesus Christ.

Just click on the question to view the answers.

How come Jesus was able to die for me? / How come only Jesus can save me and no-one else?

Is there anything I can do to save myself, without Jesus?

We are all as an unclean thing, and all our righteousnesses are as filthy rags. Isaiah 64v6. Everything we do, no matter how good, is marred by our sin and motives. Our few good actions cannot make up for our many sinful thoughts and deeds. Therefore, we can do nothing to save ourselves, but God has paid our debt for us. Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me. John 14v6. Believe on the Lord Jesus Christ, and thou shalt be saved. Acts 16v31.

If God loves me then why does he want to punish me in the first place?

We are all God’s children and He loves us! So surely when I die he’ll just forgive me anyway.
